Overview
Along with the great hit of Korean pop stars among teenagers and young adults in Hong Kong, So media conceives that K-pop lovers are a big potential market with huge extensiveness in marketing and a commercial opportunity for great exposure of products and services.

USO is an infotainment platform where lifestyle information meets with entertainment. Gathering the hottest news and music videos of Korean and overseas celebrities, stories of food and global anecdotes, USO also contains mini games, reward programmes and sticker-sharing feature.

Challenge
In this mobile-first era, K-pop fans have very few choices in their attempt to follow the latest status of their favorite stars closely. It is time-consuming and not user-friendly to browse various websites which are not responsive to mobile devices. And at the moment all the existing K-pop news app son the market are chargeable.
Solution
To equip USO as a powerful platform, we implement the campaign both vertically and horizontally. While longing for in-depth engagement with users, broadening the user base is also important.

We aim to enhance user engagement via continuous connections and a loyalty programme. Sending push notifications with relevant news and reminders of weekly events can drive users back to the app. This helps engage users in USO on a daily basis and making it part of their daily routines.

To enhance user stickiness and build the brand awareness, mini games and reward programme are integrated. Users can share their favorite posts at first hand on Facebook and earn points to win rewards at the same time. The more posts being shared, the bigger will be the chance to win. Word of Mouth is the most influential marketing tactic!

Apart from the loyalty programme, USO introduces a sticker-sharing feature to capture the great commercial potential. Being Hong Kong's first Kpop sticker-sharing platform, USO offers over 5,000 Kpop star stickers and eCards on various instant messaging tools. Users can simply select their favorite stickers on the sticker keyboard shown on IM tools.

Hosting on Amazon Web Cloud Services (AWS), AWS is powering USO scalability, availability and cost effectiveness. EC2 technology provides the app with a virtual server in the cloud. While various data centers provided by Amazon enable thousands or more of users to run the app smoothly at the same time, EC2 enables USO to scale up or down its capacity within minutes. Due to the unexpected change in number of users, on-demand instances allow our client to pay for the compute capacity they actually consume.

USO is successful in hitting a targeted group with the same need and purpose. Catering users with relevant contents keeps users being highly responsive and positive to campaigns. 87.3% of the users return to USO again. Grasping the trend at the first moment, USO has embraced most of the market share and become one of the leading brands in the field. This has attracted more and more marketers to place different types of advertisements in USO.

Insight
To capture the great number of lifestyle apps active users, we aim to build a platform for this specific community, engaging them and making it a bigger and stronger community.

Being the only one free app in the field, we provide a one-stop channel centralizing information from different websites to cater audiences’ needs. USO, a combination of the English word ‘You’ and the Cantonese word ‘So’ (meaning offers), conveying the concept of ‘Stay turned for USO and you will be rewarded.’
