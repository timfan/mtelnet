Mtel supported Retail Asia Expo 2016
Our three-day exhibition at Retail Asia Expo ended on a high note.
News, Exhibition, Expo, Sponsorship
16.06.2016
This year, we exhibited at the Retail Asia Expo at Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre again during 14-16 June! Apart from being the official app sponsor, our COO cum CTO Jeff Chau also gave a retail technology seminar. He shared his insight on how to expand clients’ market in the fast changing retail landscape on the topic ‘ Maximizing your digital strategies for market expansion’ on the first day.

We would like to thank every one of you who came along to visit our booth. We were especially honored to have inspiring conversations with retailers and professionals from different sectors. Visitors were having great fun with our interactive iPad wall, which could display our showcases and could be turned into a mini game once it was connected with a smartphone.




We hope you enjoyed our hospitality. Please feel free to contact us at info@mtelnet.com for any feedback or more information about our services.
