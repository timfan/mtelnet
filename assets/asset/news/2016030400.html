Boosting Marketing Campaign Effectiveness by Harnessing Seamless Internal Communication
Innovation in communication is not limited to external communication and marketing campaigns. Our CTO Jeff Chau has shared his insight at the AWS Digital Innovation Event.
News, Guest Speaker, Amazon Web Service
04.03.2016
Our CTO Jeff Chau spoke at the AWS Digital Innovation Series. The quarterly event is held by Amazon Web Service, where IT and marketing professionals gather to exchange bright ideas and network with peers!

Jeff has shared his insight on how effective internal communication can help business lower the cost in the long run and even bring greater revenues. He has pointed out that more and more enterprises have stepped out for implementing digital solutions in their organizations. Optimizing internal communication, workflow and information transparency is the way to go.

For more information: http://aws.amazon.com/events/digital-innovation-hk/
