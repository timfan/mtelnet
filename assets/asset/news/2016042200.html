Mtel wins 3 awards in MARKies Awards 2016
Our apps are well recognized and become the winner of Hong Kong’s premier creative awards.
News, Award
22.04.2016
We are so excited to celebrate our effort at MARKies Awards Ceremony & Gala Dinner 2016! Hosted by Marketing Magazine, the MARKies recognizes the most innovative, creative and effective campaigns or projects spanning Hong Kong’s entire marketing services industry.

This is our first year submitting MARKies Awards. We are honored to have five applications shortlisted as finalists and three awards home.



Our awards:

Starbucks Drink Builder & Pre-Order
Best Use of Mobile: Gold Award
Best Use of Apps: Gold Award

KICK OFF
Best Idea – Mobile: Bronze Award

It has been such a wonderful night for us. We look forward to developing more remarkable products in the upcoming year!
