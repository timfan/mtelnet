Mobile-First. Connect. Inspire. -- Explorium launches in Shanghai successfully
Mtel is gratified to be one of the digital solution providers for Omni-channel.
News
30.9.2015
Explorium, the first large-scale laboratory shopping mall experimenting Omni-channel ever in China celebrated grand opening on 24th September! Mtel is delighted to partner with IBM in this exciting project.

Omni-channel is a fully integrated and seamless experience to connect retailers with customers better. With the management system integrating channels on the back end, it gives retailers complete transparency to own their data for marketing strategies. It allows brands to experiment with a new business model to shape the future of the retail industry.
