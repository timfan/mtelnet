Mtel partners with VMWARE and becomes the reseller of AirWatch
We are excited to announce that Mtel has become the professional partner of VMware, as well as the official reseller of AirWatch.
News, Partnership
31.8.2015
VMware, the industry-leading virtualization software company, is well-known for technologies simplifying IT complexity and streamlining operations, helping businesses become more agile, efficient and profitable.

AirWatch opens up new sales channel with resellers. Coordinating with AirWatch technologies such as Mobile Application Management (MAM) and Mobile Device Management (MDM) on top of our app development service, Mtel now offers more comprehensive and full solutions to clients.
